Chris Farley

The tragic clown

Chris Farley in his famous "In A Van Down By the River" skit

    Timeline of Chris Farley's life:

  • 1964 - Born in Madison, WI.
  • 1986 - Graduates from Marquette University.
  • 1987..1989 - Starts comedy career, working at Ark Improv, Improv Olympic, and Second City Theatre.
  • 1990 - Announced as new Saturday Night Live cast member along with Chris Rock.
  • 1995 - Released from Saturday Night Live contract.
  • 1995 - Stars in first major movie role in Tommy Boy.
  • 1996 - Farely and TB co-star David Spade reunite for Black Sheep.
  • October 25, 1997 - Final guest appearance on SNL.
  • December 18, 1997 - Passes away from drug overdose.
  • 2005 - Posthumously awarded star on Hollywood walk of fame.

"As I stumble through this life, help me create more laughter than tears, dispense more happiness than gloom, spread more cheer than despair."

- A clown's prayer, which Farley kept in his wallet.
